Movie Industry Analysis

This is one of the projects in my data course, Data Systems for Data Analytics. It is a comprehensive analysis and modeling project that explores the film industry, including producers, film stars, and past and present films. Using the Python language to process data and PowerBI to observe the trends of movies over the years, I focus on understanding the diversity of genres as well as the influence of holiday movies on the film industry worldwide. From there, it can bring benefits to related parties, the insights could be invaluable in making strategic decisions regarding film production, marketing, casting, and distribution.

Spotify Music Analysis

This is a project using the R programming language to produce data analysis and visualizations that allow track creators to efficiently see how musical qualities interact to enable better-published mixes and make listeners' musical preferences known. At the same time, it estimates the popularity status of musical genres based on the characteristics of the audio materials used in them by using different classification methods in the Scikit-learning.

NASA L'SPACE Mission Concept Academy Summer 2023

This is the project that I participated in the summer of 2023 at NASA L'PACE organized by Arizona State University. Throughout the program, I worked in an interdisciplinary team of 10 members as we collaborated and grappled with the challenge of designing a lander to further the science related to the semester's mission, and were expected to develop and present a comprehensive Preliminary Design Review (PDR) document. As a Program Analyst, I have the opportunity to improve my Excel skills in building a cost optimization model for the Ernutet Crater Model to ensure the project to be completed successfully and within a budget of $400M.

House Prices Prediction

This is my personal coding project about predicting house price trends in the US based on the amenities of each house. By utilizing SQL libraries, exploratory data analysis (EDA), Pandas, Seaborn, and TensorFlow tools, my goal is to understand how individual house characteristics contribute to house price differences. Data for analytics is collected on Kaggle.
